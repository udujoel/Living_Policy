Universal Urban Greenery Initiative 2024

Objectives:
The primary goal is to increase urban canopy coverage in major metropolitan areas by 25% over the next 15 years. This will be achieved through a combination of mandatory green roofs on new commercial buildings and a 15% subsidy for residential vertical gardens.

Levers:
- Green Roof Mandate: Current coverage requirement is 40% of roof area.
- Vertical Garden Subsidy: 15% of installation costs.
- Target Cities: Currently focused on top 5 most densely populated cities.

Constraints:
- Budget: Initial funding cap of $2.5 billion.
- Legal: Requires zoning law amendments in 3 out of 5 cities.

Stakeholders:
- Urban residents
- Real estate developers
- Landscaping industry
- Municipal planning committees
