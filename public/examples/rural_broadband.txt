Rural Connectivity and Digital Equity Act

Goals:
Ensure 99% broadband penetration in rural counties (defined as <100 persons/sq mile) by 2030. The target speed is 100/20 Mbps.

Key Levers:
- Infrastructure Grant: $5000 per new household connection.
- Spectrum Tax Credit: 20% reduction for providers covering "dead zones".
- Device Voucher: $200 for low-income rural households.

Constraints:
- Geographic: Topographic challenges in Appalachian and Rocky Mountain regions.
- Time: 6-year rollout period.

Impacted Populations:
- Rural students
- Small-scale farmers
- Telehealth providers
- Regional ISPs
